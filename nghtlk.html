Description

     NGHTLK, short for Night Talk, is a series of works made by sleepless person and inspired by the midnight mood. The series is purely personal and was created to express my artistic urge.

The series began in 2021 with three illustrations named "Oops", "Dance Tonight" and "I'm Drunk!". It's fun to play with their shapes and colors. I then continued to draw and animate my characters - cats in different contexts that my emotions go through.
